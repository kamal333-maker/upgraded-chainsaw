The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the larger foxhound. The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the Beagle is employed as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. It is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. The Beagle’s sense of smell is one of the strongest among dog breeds.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting small game like rabbits.
  3. Beagles have a distinct howl-like bark called a “bay.”
  4. The popular cartoon dog Snoopy is a Beagle.
  5. Beagles are known for being friendly and curious companions.
